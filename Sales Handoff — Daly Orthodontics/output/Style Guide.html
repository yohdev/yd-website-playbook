01 / Foundations

The brand in one line.

A specialist orthodontic practice in Burlington, NC. Logo and base palette are locked. Typography, voice, UI, and imagery are a considered reinvention — restrained, editorial, confidence-forward. The gold logo sets the register; the system carries the mood.

Brand posture

Locked

  • Primary logo lockup (gold-on-dark horizontal)
  • Deep green (#1C3A27) + warm gold (#C9A633) as anchor palette
  • Orthodontics-only messaging positioning

Open — reinvented here

  • Display + body typography (Fraunces + Inter)
  • UI treatments, CTAs, cards, forms
  • Voice, imagery, layout composition

13 / Do / Don't

Rules with receipts.

The current site breaks most of these. The redesign is a promise to hold the line.

Do

Use the Daly Gold (#C9A633) as the only gold in the system. Small accents, link underlines, iconography.

Pair cream pills on green backgrounds. Pair green solid on cream backgrounds. Keep CTA hierarchy flat.

Lead hero with a declarative statement in Fraunces. Photography can live below.

Use Dr. Daly as the proper noun for the doctor. Never "our team" when one person is meant.

Don't

Don't substitute a mustard yellow (#B9A628 or similar). It dulls the logo and reads amateur.

Don't run more than two floating chrome elements at once. The current site's four-widget chrome is retired.

Don't use stock photography in the hero. If custom photography isn't available, go typographic.

Don't borrow DTC health voice ("hey there!"). The brand is warm, not casual. Plainspoken, not cheeky.
