Questions we hear every week

Start here.

Do you take my insurance?

We accept most major dental insurance plans with orthodontic coverage. Bring your card to the consult and we'll run the benefits for you on the spot — no guessing.

How much do braces or aligners cost?

It depends on the complexity of your case. We'll give you a fixed, all-in price in writing at the consult, with monthly payment options. You'll never get a bill for something we didn't quote.

My child is seven. Is that too young?

Seven is actually the age the American Association of Orthodontists recommends for a first evaluation. Most kids don't need treatment yet, but a quick check tells us whether early intervention would save you money and time later.

Do you do cleanings, fillings, or extractions?

No. We're an orthodontics-only specialty practice. If your treatment plan requires a dental procedure (like an extraction), we'll coordinate with your regular dentist.

How long will treatment take?

Most adult aligner cases run 6–18 months. Traditional braces average 18–24 months. Your exact timeline depends on your bite — Dr. Daly will give you a real estimate at your consult.

Can I come for a second opinion?

Please do. The consultation is complimentary whether you treat with us or not. Bring records from your current provider and we'll tell you honestly what we'd do differently, if anything.
