# Discovery Brief — Daly Orthodontics

Daly Orthodontics is a private orthodontics practice approximately one year into operation in Burlington, North Carolina, serving local patients seeking braces, aligners, and related orthodontic treatment. Victor Daniel Daly (co-owner, business side) and his wife (the practicing orthodontist) are seeking to exit their current vendor, Pearl Sites, before the June 2026 billing cycle to reclaim full ownership of their website assets and eliminate a dependency that has cost them money, autonomy, and search relevance. The immediate need is a lift-and-shift migration from the Pearl Sites CMS to WordPress, preserving existing SEO equity and cleaning up mobile navigation, with a full UX redesign scoped as a separate Milestone 2. A win looks like: the site is live on WordPress before June 2026, the client cancels Pearl Sites without paying for another month, Google treats it as the same site, and the client can manage their own content going forward without a gatekeeper.

## Scope

**In scope**

- Lift-and-shift migration of current site from Pearl Sites CMS to WordPress (Milestone 1)
- 100% client ownership of all website assets, content, and infrastructure post-migration
- Pre-migration SEO scan to baseline existing rankings and identify high-value pages
- One-for-one URL and content structure migration to preserve backlink equity and prevent Google reindexing the site as new
- Evaluation of existing blog content (approximately one post per week dating back to 2025) with migration of relevant posts
- Minor mobile polish and navigation cleanup — mobile nav currently identified as crowded and poorly designed
- Native WordPress block builder implementation — no third-party page builder plugins
- Hosting setup: either YohDev-managed hosting with a clearly defined exit process, or client-selected host with YohDev support (to be confirmed)
- Bi-weekly progress meetings with screen-share reviews throughout the project
- Full UX redesign scoped and quoted separately as Milestone 2 (future phase, timeline TBD)
- Optional future SEO content strategy to be scoped separately

**Out of scope**

- Full UX redesign is explicitly excluded from Milestone 1 due to hard June 2026 deadline
- SEO content strategy and blog writing are not contracted as part of Milestone 1
- Google Ads management is not in current scope

---

## Success criteria

1. Site is fully migrated to WordPress and live before the June 2026 Pearl Sites billing date, enabling the client to cancel their Pearl Sites subscription without paying for an additional month.
2. Client holds 100% ownership of their domain, content, and site infrastructure with no remaining vendor dependency on Pearl Sites.
3. Google reads the migrated site as the same site — no meaningful loss of existing search rankings or backlink equity after migration.
4. Client can independently add pages, update content, and manage the site without routing requests through a third-party vendor.
5. Mobile navigation is clean, functional, and user-friendly on completion of Milestone 1.
