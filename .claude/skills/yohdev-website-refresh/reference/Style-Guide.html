// 01  color

Black and teal, anchored by a neutral scale.

Black does the heavy lifting; teal carries the brand accent on CTAs and highlights. A full 9-step neutral scale handles everything else. Slugs map directly to theme.json → settings.color.palette.

A11y
Body copy (Ink #171717) on White passes WCAG AAA. Teal (#00B4B4) on White fails AA for text at 16px — use it only as a surface (on CTAs with Black text), never as foreground text on light backgrounds. For text, use Teal Deep (#009494) which passes AA Large.
